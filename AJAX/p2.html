promises

what is promise?

promise is a built in constructor of javascript.

a promise object represents an operation which has produced or will eventually produce a value promises provide a robust way to wrap the possibility pending result of asynchronous request

states of promises

1. pending

the underlying operation has not yet completed or neither rejected

2. fullfilled

the operation has finishedd and promise is fullfilled with a response. (.then()) is used handle fulfilled responses.

3. rejected

the error occured during the operation is required with a reason .(.catch()) is uesd to catch the error.

4. settled

a promise a said to settled, (either resolved or rejected) once the promise is settled , it becomes immutable.
